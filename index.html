About Me

Jasmine McCloud is a traditional artist and a freelance graphic designer from the Inland Empire. She has been painting for 12 years, drawing for over 20 years and has been doing graphic design work for 5 years. After she graduated high school in 2005 she began attending Chaffey Community College where she took mostly art history courses. She is now attending Mt Sierra College where she is obtaining a bachelors degree in Graphic Design. After she finishes at Mt Sierra College she hopes to continue making traditional art as well as graphic design work.
